About CivicTrack

What is CivicTrack?

CivicTrack is a citizen grievance management portal that enables residents to report public issues directly to the responsible authorities.

The platform helps bridge the gap between citizens and government departments by providing a simple and transparent complaint tracking system.

Our Mission

To empower citizens to actively participate in improving their communities through responsible reporting and civic engagement.

Our Vision

To create cleaner, safer, greener and more sustainable communities through technology and collaboration.

How CivicTrack Works

  1. User registers and logs into CivicTrack.
  2. User submits a complaint regarding a public issue.
  3. Complaint is stored in the system and becomes visible to authorities.
  4. Authorities review and update complaint status.
  5. User can track progress from their My Complaints page.
  6. Once resolved, the complaint is marked as Resolved.

Issues That Can Be Reported

  • Road Damage
  • Water Supply Issues
  • Street Light Problems
  • Garbage Accumulation
  • Public Safety Concerns
  • Environmental Issues
  • Other Community Problems
