Guidelines for a Better Community

Protect Public Property

Public property such as roads, parks, street lights, bus stops and government buildings belong to everyone. Citizens should use them responsibly and report any damages through CivicTrack.

Environmental Responsibility

Protect nature by reducing pollution, planting trees, avoiding plastic waste and participating in community clean-up programs.

Waste Management

  • Dispose waste only in designated bins.
  • Separate recyclable and non-recyclable waste.
  • Avoid littering in public places.
  • Support recycling initiatives.

Water Conservation

  • Report water leakages immediately.
  • Use water responsibly.
  • Avoid unnecessary wastage.
  • Promote rainwater harvesting.

Community Safety

  • Report damaged roads and street lights.
  • Report unsafe public areas.
  • Follow traffic regulations.
  • Support emergency services.

Responsible Citizenship

CivicTrack encourages every citizen to actively participate in improving society by reporting genuine issues and helping authorities create a safer and cleaner environment.
